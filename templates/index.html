Introducing MUSIQ, your gateway to a world of harmonious melodies, rhythmic beats, and endless musical possibilities. Dive into the enchanting realm of sound with our cutting-edge music streaming app that brings your favorite tunes right to your fingertips. Tune in, turn up the volume, and let MUSIQ be the soundtrack to your life. Let the journey begin!

Features

This is a CRUD Application done as a part of Modern Application Development I, MAD-I capstone project. It has features of Song/Album Management, and Playlist Management.

Technologies Used

The front-end is developed using HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript, while the back-end is implemented with the Flask framework. The database utilized is SQLite3.
